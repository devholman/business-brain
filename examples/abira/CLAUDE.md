## Business at a Glance

- **What:** Custom-designed team apparel for youth and recreational softball teams. Players and parents order individually through a team store (Abira Catalog) — no coach chasing down sizes or money.
- **Stage:** Early growth (under $1,000/month revenue)
- **Team:** Owner + 1 part-time helper
- **Open Problem #1:** Client base has not grown beyond word-of-mouth — no executed method for obtaining new clients
