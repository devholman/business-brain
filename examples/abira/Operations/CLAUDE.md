## Your Focus

Order tracking, production coordination, fulfillment, vendor management.

**The production pipeline:**
1. Client confirms order → design finalized
2. Heat transfers ordered from local vendor (2-day pickup)
3. Blank garments ordered simultaneously
4. Both arrive → in-house pressing → QC → package by individual
5. Fulfillment: bulk local pickup or paid shipping via Abira Catalog

Target turnaround: 7–8 days total. Both transfers and blanks must arrive together.
